Put your Meta-Pacs to Work to earn $PAC and $CHERRY

A 30 days peace treaty has been made before PacWorld Gaming Universe opens up for the Final Battle. In preparation of the Final Battle, Lords can stake their Meta-Pacs in our staking mechenism and earn in-game currencies, $PAC and $CHERRY. These currencies are used to buy tools, accessories, upgradeable, and mint GEN1 mints. Equip yourself with these tools and get a head start against other Lords once PacWorld Gaming Universe opens up. The staking game will last for exactly 30 days after the mint of Meta-Pacs. PacWorld Gaming Universe will open up once the staking game has ended.
